    Discuss what it is and why it is relevant.

  • Growth mindset refers to an individual's ability and intelligence that can be developed and improved over a period of time through effort, perseverance, and learning. It’s incredibly relevant because without a growth mindset, you’ll end up having a ‘fixed mindset’ which believes that things can’t change or better themselves. By introducing growth mindset, it promotes a more positive and proactive approach to learning, challenges, and personal development. It allows for a belief that everything can be taken another step above rather than sitting at a plateau.
